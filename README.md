## üìÑ Research Articles and Reviews

Key articles and reviews for learning proteomics and mass spectrometry fundamentals:

### General Proteomics
- **[A Beginner's Guide to Mass Spectrometry-Based Proteomics](https://portlandpress.com/biochemist/article/42/5/64/226371/A-beginner-s-guide-to-mass-spectrometry-based)** 
- **[Proteomics Tutorial](https://jessegmeyerlab.github.io/proteomics-tutorial/v/505a3f0680a2e638e6bf04369d273e42e1a162ae/)**
- **[Intensive BU-proteomics introduction](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/full/10.1021/acsmeasuresciau.3c00068?ref=utm_source%3Dpubsw&utm_campaign=IC004_ST0004D_T000549_OA_Microsite_LP&src=IC004_ST0004D_T000549_OA_Microsite_LP&utm_medium=web)** 
- **[Intro to Proteomics](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jproteome.2c00838)** - Pedagogical introduction to MS-based proteomics

### Educational Approaches
- **[Teaching Mass Spectrometry](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jchemed.0c00853)** - Journal of Chemical Education (2021)
- **[Interdisciplinary Mass Spectrometry Curriculum](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/ed086p966)** - Journal of Chemical Education (2009)
- **[Computational Proteomics Education](https://www.worldscientific.com/doi/abs/10.1142/S0219720012310026)** - Journal of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology (2012)

### Mass Spectrometry
- **[Electrospray Ionization: We Taught Elephants to Fly](https://portlandpress.com/biochemist/article/44/5/5/232008/Electrospray-ionization-We-taught-elephants-to-fly)** - The Biochemist (2022) - History and principles of ESI
- **[Mass Spectrometry for Proteomics](https://www.nature.com/articles/nature01511)** - Nature (2003) - Amazign review by Ruedi Aebersold & Matthias Mann
- **[High-resolution mass spectrometry](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/chapter/edited-volume/abs/pii/B9780128199671000165?via%3Dihub)** - Book chapter
- **[Mass Spectrometry from a computational Lense](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/1471-2105-15-S7-S9)** - A mass spectrometry tutorial from a computer scientist's point of view


### Clinical Applications
- **[Clinical Proteomics](https://www.jcehepatology.com/article/S0973-6883(19)30127-6/abstract)** - Journal of Clinical and Experimental Hepatology (2019)
- **[Proteomics in Medicine](https://www.mdpi.com/2227-7382/8/3/14)** - Proteomes (2020)

### Protein/Peptides Identification and Data Interpretation

- **[Database searching of MS Data](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acs.jchemed.0c00853)** - Journal of Chemical Education (2021)
